🚀 INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Simple 3-Step Installation:

Step 1: Load Extension (2 minutes)
  1. Open Google Chrome
  2. Visit: chrome://extensions/
  3. Click "Developer mode" toggle (top right)
  4. Click "Load unpacked"
  5. Select: d:\dtx8\chatgpt-extension
  6. Click "Select Folder"
  → Extension loads! Icon appears in toolbar

Step 2: Configure (1 minute)
  1. Click the extension icon in toolbar
  2. Click "Cấu hình" (Settings) tab
  3. Enter your prompt in the text area
     Example: "Hãy giải thích AI là gì?"
  4. (Optional) Enable auto-run and set interval
  5. Click "Lưu cấu hình" (Save Configuration)

Step 3: Test (30 seconds)
  1. Click extension icon again
  2. Click "Kết quả" (Results) tab
  3. Click "Chạy ngay" (Run Now) button
  4. Wait 10 seconds for ChatGPT to respond
  5. View results in the display area
  → Done! 🎉
